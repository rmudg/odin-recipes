Homeade Lasagna

Description:

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 onces ricotta cheese
  • 4 ounces of parmesan cheese
  • 24 ounces of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on boil (1 tbsp salt for every 2 quarts of water)
  2. Brown the ground beef using 2 teapsoons of olive oil
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook half a minute more
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain lasagna noodles
  6. In a 9x13-inch casserole or lasagna dish, ladle a cup of sauce and spread it over the bottom of the dish
  7. Sprinkle a layer of grated mozzarella on top of lasagna sauce
  8. Apply second layer of noodles. Top with remaining sauce
  9. Finish with another layer of noodles. Spread the reamining sauce over the top layer of noodles
  10. Bake at 375 degrees fahrenheit
  11. Serve!
